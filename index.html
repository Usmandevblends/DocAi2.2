About DocAI

DocAI is a very lightweight technology and it can easily be installed with a click of a button. In less than 5 minutes you can install DocAI and to start using it for free. We have also designed DocAI in a way that doesn’t require any training. Once you have DocAI installed, the Ambient Scribe, Medical Dictation, Medical Search (and EHR Assistant on OSCAR Pro) skills are available immediately and are very intuitive and simple to use.
